What devices do you shoot on?

We deploy standard premium setups including high-speed mirrorless systems like the Sony Alpha Series with customized G-Master prime lenses, alongside top-tier Apple iPhone Pro platforms paired with high-end anamorphic visual rigs for mobile cinematography.

What is the typical deliverable turnaround time?

Previews of selected frames are shared inside 72 hours from the shoot date. The complete finalized suite of cinematic, color-graded deliverables is completed and delivered inside 14-21 working days.

Do you travel out of state for sessions?

Absolutely. We serve regional projects, but we love executing lifestyle, editorial, and bike shoots anywhere across India and international destinations. Custom logistics charges are applied to off-grid projects.
